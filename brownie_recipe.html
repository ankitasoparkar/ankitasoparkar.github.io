Brownie with Chocolate Chips

Ingredients

  1. Dark Chocolate - Jindal brand
  2. Unsalted butter-cut into pieces 1/2 cup or 113 grams
  3. Cocoa powder (wakefield) 15gm/2 tablespoon
  4. Eagle brand Castor sugar - 200 gm
  5. Normal vanilla extract - 1 teaspoon
  6. Eggs - 3 large
  7. Maida - 95 gm
  8. Salt - 1/4 teaspoon
  9. White chocolate chips - 100 gm
